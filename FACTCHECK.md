## 1. Pentagon 2025 report — "national total war" / "clash of national systems" — ✅
The Dec 23, 2025 *Annual Report to Congress on Military and Security Developments Involving the PRC* describes the PLA's vision of conflict as a "clash of national systems" and a whole-of-nation mobilization termed "national total war." Confirmed across [USNI News](https://news.usni.org/2025/12/24/pentagon-annual-report-on-chinese-military-and-security-developments-2), [Task & Purpose](https://taskandpurpose.com/video/china-national-total-war/), and [The Defense Watch](https://thedefensewatch.com/policy-strategy/pentagons-2025-china-military-report/). Report PDF: [media.defense.gov](https://media.defense.gov/2025/Dec/23/2003849070/-1/-1/1/ANNUAL-REPORT-TO-CONGRESS-MILITARY-AND-SECURITY-DEVELOPMENTS-INVOLVING-THE-PEOPLES-REPUBLIC-OF-CHINA-2025.PDF).

## 2. PLA training with civilian roll-on/roll-off vessels — ✅
In the 2025 report: PLAN "continues to train with civilian roll-on/roll-off commercial vessels," likely to mitigate its amphibious-lift shortfall; mid-2025 deck-cargo-ship beach landings noted. Sources: [USNI News](https://news.usni.org/2025/12/24/pentagon-annual-report-on-chinese-military-and-security-developments-2), [Army Recognition](https://www.armyrecognition.com/news/navy-news/2025/intel-china-converts-civilian-fleet-into-wartime-force-to-support-rapid-taiwan-invasion).

## 3. DeepSeek "50,000 GPUs stockpiled" — 🔧 reattributed
The Cicero draft attributed this to the Pentagon report; I could not confirm the figure appears there. The figure originates with analyst estimates (SemiAnalysis: ~50K Hopper-generation GPUs, a mix of H800/H100/H20) and the [House Select Committee on the CCP's April 2025 DeepSeek investigation](https://chinaselectcommittee.house.gov/sites/evo-subsites/selectcommitteeontheccp.house.gov/files/evo-media-document/DeepSeek%20Final.pdf); [Reuters/CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/06/24/deepseek-aids-chinas-military-and-evaded-export-controls-us-official-says-reuters.html) reported U.S. officials saying DeepSeek aids China's military and evaded export controls, but could not verify chip counts. **Draft now says "congressional investigators and independent analysts estimate."** Added (verified): PLA entities referenced DeepSeek models in at least a dozen 2025 military procurement tenders ([CNBC/Reuters](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/06/24/deepseek-aids-chinas-military-and-evaded-export-controls-us-official-says-reuters.html)).

## 4. Rare earths "China controls 90%" — 🔧 precised
Correct for *processing/refining* (~90% global share), not mining or reserves. F-35: 900+ lbs of rare-earth materials per aircraft. Sources: [CSIS](https://www.csis.org/analysis/chinas-new-rare-earth-and-magnet-restrictions-threaten-us-defense-supply-chains), [Modern War Institute](https://mwi.westpoint.edu/minerals-magnets-and-military-capability-chinas-rare-earth-weaponization-should-be-a-wake-up-call/), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/10/14/china-trump-xi-rare-earth-defense-critical-mineral-trade-war-tariffs.html). Draft now says "roughly 90% of the world's rare-earth processing... over 900 pounds."
